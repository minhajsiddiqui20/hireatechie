The engagement model

Project-based, not permanent

Every engagement is structured around the work, not around creating ongoing employment obligations for your business.

On-site in NZ or AU

For projects that need someone physically present, the professional travels to your location for the duration. Engagements typically run three months to one year, sometimes longer for the right project. This is the most common model for our clients.

Remote from home country

If you prefer remote delivery, the professional works from their home country. This suits well-defined technical workstreams where the team is already distributed or the work is self-contained.

Hybrid arrangements

Some engagements start remotely and move to on-site as the project evolves. We are flexible and will structure the arrangement around what actually makes the work go well.

For IT professionals

If you are an IT professional in India interested in project-based work with NZ and AU clients, here is exactly what you can expect.

Create a profile in our talent network. No cost, no commitment. We review every profile and reach out when a matching opportunity comes up.

Rates are set at a level that reflects the international market you are serving. You earn significantly more than you would for equivalent work at home. When the project ends, you return home unless a new project continues.
